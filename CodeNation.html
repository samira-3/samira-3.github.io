Brooklyn, New York, United States

Code Nation mobilize the tech community to help more young people access careers in tech. With a volunteer teaching corps of 300 technology professionals and a powerful network of school and company partners, we bring coding courses and work-based learning programs to students who attend under-resourced high schools. That means students leave Code Nation programs career-ready, with a holistic set of skills, competencies, and resources that set them up for success.


  • To get students excited about coding and introduce them to the field of Computer Science.
  • Equip students with foundational technical skills in web development, focusing on HTML, CSS, jQuery, and JavaScript.
  • Spark an interest in tech careers through creative coding projects and field trips.
  • The structure contains 7 instructional units, each centered around a project.
  • In one semester classes focus on Units 1-4, with a minimum of 23 hours of instruction. Totaling around 30 lessons.
